# BeatGenome 🧬🎛️

**Learn and explore Electronic Music Culture — one genre at a time.**
By DJ7 · Wilsonlicioussss

Hey — I'm Wilson, and BeatGenome is my love letter to electronic music culture, shared with everyone.

Electronic music is so much more than a sound. It's decades of culture: warehouse parties and festival mainstages, pirate radio and bedroom producers, scenes born in Detroit, Chicago, Berlin, London and Goa that grew into hundreds of genres — each with its own tempo, mood and story. If you've ever wondered how techno relates to house, what actually separates drum & bass from dubstep, or why a great DJ set feels like a journey, BeatGenome was made for you.

It's a free, interactive map of **159 genres and subgenres**, running right in your browser. Nothing to install, no sign-up, no ads. Just open it and start wandering.

## What you can learn and explore

**See the culture as a living map.** Every genre is a glowing node in a connected universe — grouped into families, linked by lineage (which genre gave birth to which), by fusion, and by what DJs actually blend together. Drag, zoom, tap. The shape of the scene reveals itself the longer you explore.

**Hear what makes each genre tick.** Turn on sound and every genre plays its own living loop — tempo, groove, drums, bassline, chords — generated live, so you can *hear* the difference between deep house and tech house instead of just reading about it.

**Follow the timeline.** Switch to the DNA view and the genres arrange themselves by era, like a strand of musical DNA — from disco's echoes to today's hyperpop and phonk.

**Understand the DJ craft.** Each genre carries real mixing intel: BPM ranges, keys in Camelot notation, which genres blend well, transition tips and FX moves — the invisible skills behind a seamless set.

**Peek into the producer's studio.** Drum patterns, basslines, chord progressions, sound-design notes — the building blocks of each sound, with little players so you can hear the ideas, not just read them.

**Play with the connections.** Compare two genres side by side, morph one into another and listen to the blend, or filter the whole map by mood — dark, euphoric, dreamy, whatever you're chasing.

**Take it anywhere.** Built for phones and tablets too — pinch to zoom, tap a genre, and its story slides up while the music keeps playing.

**Go deeper.** Built-in guides cover harmonic mixing, set building, FX and loops, and live performance — a small library of craft knowledge for the curious.

## Honest by design

BeatGenome is made to be fun and genuinely educational, not the final word. The genre map is grounded in real taxonomy; tempos, keys and structures are informed conventions; the artists and tracks are classic pointers, not a live chart. And there's no tracking, no ads, no cookies — your colour theme is the only thing remembered, and it never leaves your device.

### For the curious — run or host it yourself

BeatGenome is a single, self-contained web app (vanilla JavaScript + HTML5 Canvas, no frameworks, no build step). Open `index.html` in any modern browser, or host it free: upload this folder to a public GitHub repo (with `index.html` at the root) and enable **Settings → Pages → Deploy from a branch**. Optional sound is procedural via Tone.js — no audio files, no samples.
